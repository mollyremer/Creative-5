About Zilco Tech

Zilco Tech strives to create time-saving, stress-releaving automated solutions to common daily tasks businesses complete daily on computers. We can automate finance input; daily, weekly, monthly, or even yearly expenses and revenue wherever your business keeps its data, such as on quickbooks. We can also automate the sending of emails, deleting or updating data on online stores, logging data input, just about anything your business does on a computer.

We serve a number of businesses in the Utah, and Arizona areas. We're responsible for automating the billing process of Greater Anaesthesia solutions, an anaesthesia provider for hospitals and clinics in the Mesa Arizona area. Ideas of how we can help your business could include searching and recording client/customer information on data software, automating the extracting infromation from competitors' websites, and so much more.
